<h3 align="center">🧪 What We're Building</h3>

<p align="center"><strong>Neo RP v1</strong><br>
A next-gen roleplay framework, built from zero — no templates, no hand-me-down logic.
</p>

<ul align="center">
  <li>🔒 <strong>One-character system</strong> – No alts. Your identity matters.</li>
  <li>🎯 <strong>Rolelocked weapons & admin-run factions</strong> – Structured power, balanced gameplay.</li>
  <li>⚖️ <strong>Legal & illegal economy loops</strong> – Immersive, grindable, and reactive to the world.</li>
  <li>🖥️ <strong>Narrative-first UI</strong> – Interfaces tell a story, not just show stats.</li>
  <li>💻 <strong>Custom in-game OS</strong> – A fully integrated player desktop, built into RP.</li>
</ul>
